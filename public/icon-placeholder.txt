ICON PLACEHOLDERS NEEDED

Create the following icon files for your PWA:

1. icon-192x192.png (192×192 pixels)
2. icon-512x512.png (512×512 pixels)  
3. apple-touch-icon.png (180×180 pixels)

You can create these using:
- Canva (free online tool)
- Figma
- Adobe Illustrator
- Or any online PWA icon generator

Recommended design:
- Simple, bold icon
- High contrast
- Recognizable at small sizes
- Represents fitness/workout theme
- Use your brand colors
